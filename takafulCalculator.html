Why Takaful is so important?

Takaful is essential in providing a financial safety net and acts as an income protection/replacement to take care of yourself and your family in the event of something unexpected such as death and total permanent disability (TPD).

Our expertise to help you to maximize the benefit of takaful beyond the normal practice. You can specifically manage how your sum covered to be managed and spent to take care of yourself and your family with your specific instruction as well to grow your values of sum covered with investment plan for long term benefit. Interesting!

What are the differences between Takaful and Insurance?

Both takaful and conventional insurance provide protection in the event of unforeseen events. But Takaful provides protection based on Shariah principles. All investments managed by the takaful operator are made in accordance with the Shariah law.

Contributions from the participants in Takaful will be invested into Shariah compliant funds to derive investment income and it is important to ensure that the ‘income’ or ‘profit’ form the investment which will be shared among the participant is ‘halal’.

Am I eligible to participate in Takaful?

If you have been diagnosed with a critical illness you are not eligible to participate in Takaful. It is important to plan early when you are still healthy and sound mind to be covered with Takaful.

What is the triggering event that Takaful can be claimed?

The sum covered of your Takaful will be payout by the Takaful provider in the event of death, total permanent disability (TPD) and when you diagnosed with a critical illness (CI).

Upon death, the sum covered of your Takaful will be pay out immediately to your nominee/beneficiaries. If you diagnosed with critical illness or TPD the sum covered will be pay out to you or your nominee/beneficiaries.
