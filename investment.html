To Invest or to Protect? Why Not Both?

What does it take to waste RM3,600 a year? Only RM10 a day spent on miscellaneous. What could be the RM10 you spend unnecessarily a day? It could be a pack of cigarette, list of snacks from chips, ice-cream, chocolates and cookies; lifestyle drinks of boba teas alike and a lot more items you don’t necessarily need to spend.

According to a statistic conducted by Ministry of Finance, Malaysians spend 31.2% of their salary on food, which is about RM20 to RM50 a day. Do you know with that RM10 a day you spend on miscellaneous, you could protect yourself against life uncertainties such as death, disability, accident, illness and on top of that earn potential return from investment fund? Let’s find out how.

What is Investment-Linked Takaful?

A Family Takaful plan that combines investment to build savings fund from the potential returns and protection coverage against death, disability, critical illness and accidental as well as hospitalisation benefit. Part of the contributions will be invested in a variety of Shariah-approved investment funds of your choice.

Why should I participate in Investment-Linked Takaful plan and what are the benefits?

  • Best of both worlds – Get yourself protected and enjoy potential returns in due as a safety net for retirement or fulfilling other life goals.
  • Flexible – allows you to customise your own level of protection and investment to suit your affordability. You may also choose to switch investment funds within the Takaful Operator’s fund options depending on your risk appetite.
  • Wholesome coverage – you may expect coverage to include death, critical illness, hospitalisation, accidental death, income protection and total & permanent disability with coverage amount varies based on your needs.
  • Partial withdrawals – you can make withdrawal any time should you need financial aid in case of emergencies.

Disability Coverage Up to Maturity of Certificate*
Takaful ProInvest provides Total and Permanent Disability (TPD) support up to age 75 and Golden Age Disability (GAD) will provides you an additional disability coverage up to age 100. In the event of TPD or GAD and if such disability persists for at least 6 months, 100% of BSC and PIA value will be payable.

Family Care Privilege
Takaful ProInvest provides protection for up to 4 of your legal spouses and/or children with the coverage amount of up to 25% of BSC without medical underwriting, subject to a maximum of RM300,000 per life.
