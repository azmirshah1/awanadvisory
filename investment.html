To Invest or to Protect? Why Not Both?

The unpredictability of how life events unfold is a constant reminder that we should always be prepared. Sufficient and stable savings are necessary to provide security to our loved ones in the event of an unforeseen incident. The potential growth of our savings can further assist us in the pursuit of our life goals and financial stability.

What is Investment-Linked Takaful?

An investment-linked takaful is a family takaful plan that combines investment and takaful cover. Your contribution will provide takaful cover, which includes death and disability benefits, and part of the contributions will be invested in a variety of Shariah-approved investment funds of your choice.

"You Have the Flexibility in Building Up Your Protection and Savings"

You have the flexibility to choose the combination of payment and coverage duration to suit your protection needs. Further enhance your protection and savings by adding different riders and allocate part of your contribution to investment.

Maximise the Return of Your Money

We can help you to get a savings plan that offers Takaful coverage as well as potential investment growth, allowing you to maximise the return of your money.

Why should I participate in this plan?

  • Investment Booster – You can grow your Account Value with a yearly Investment Booster that will be credited into your Participant’s Investment Fund. The amount will then be invested according to the fund selected.
  • Maturity Booster – allows you to customise your own level of protection and investment to suit your affordability. You may also choose to switch investment funds within the Takaful Operator’s fund options depending on your risk appetite.
  • Savings Booster – Rewards you for good financial habits when you ensure that your payments are up to date with no withdrawal from your Participant’s Savings Fund.
  • Partial withdrawals – you can make withdrawal any time should you need financial aid in case of emergencies.

Life Coverage
If you pass away or suffer from TPD, you or your family will receive a lump sum payment of the eligible benefits.

Easy Enrollment
No medical check-up is required when you participate in this plan with an Annual Contribution of up to RM50,000.

This is a Shariah-compliant plan which consists of Ordinary Family Takaful and investment-linked portions which provide you with the following benefits: Investment Booster, Maturity Booster and Savings Booster. It also offers you the potential of upside investment gains and the flexibility in managing your savings.

This plan available for individuals aged between 14 days old until 60 years old.
