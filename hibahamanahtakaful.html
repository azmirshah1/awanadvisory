All you need to know about Hibah Amanah Takful

Giving a Hibah as a lasting gift from you

Takaful is a consensus of a group of individuals to insure each other and help each other to meet certain needs agreed upon among them such as providing compensation for a disaster that befell as well as other financial needs. In takaful, there are participants who choose hibah takaful as one of the ways they help and protect their families when they are no longer able to give their best for their loved ones. Here, we will explain what a takaful plan is and what are the benefits of choosing the best takaful plan.

What is a Hibah Takaful?

You realize that life has its ups and downs, and you want to be prepared for it. So go ahead with your intentions and do your best for those you love, whether for your children, or anyone for example to an orphan. Many takaful owners make the choice to grant takaful as their last resort.

Hibah takaful is the loving gift of property to anyone who is desired without expecting a return during life in the form of takaful compensation. It was first introduced in the takaful industry after the Islamic Financial Services Act 2013 came into existence. The grant to be received by the recipient will be effective after the death of the Certificate holder.

Advantages of Hibah Takaful?


1. Facilitate Debt Settlement

If there are debts or bills that are in arrears at the time of your death, the grants you give to family members please them to pay off any payments and debts you have.

2. In Lieu of Income

This advantage is clear and beneficial for you if you are the main breadwinner of the family. If anything happens to you, the takaful hibah given to the heirs is able to bear the family income while they manage the remains and settle any of your worldly matters.

3. Can be donated to non -heirs of Faraid

Hibah takaful is one of the alternatives to faraid because it can be given to heirs, family members or to anyone who is desired.

Who Should Take a Hibah Takaful?

On average, donating takaful is a beneficial thing for everyone who contributes to takaful. Here we state the most beneficial individuals to donate takaful to their loved ones.

  • Couples with adopted children.
  • Husbands who have wives.
  • Have only daughters.
  • Having more sons than daughters.
  • Couples with no children.
  • Having a disabled child.
  • A child who still has a mother and father.
  • Businessman.
  • Individuals who remarry.
  • Giving to non -Muslim family members.

3 important things to look for before choosing a Hibah Takaful plan

Protect Yourself

Hibah Takaful / Life Takaful is not limited to protecting your family but is also allows you to protect yourself in case of any emergency that causes any partial or permanent disability.

No limit

The takaful policy is not limited to be distributed lump sum or in full amount directly to the beneficiary in the unfortunate event of death or permanent disability.

Double the value

You are allowed to double the value of the benefit takaful policy for personal use and beneficiary.
